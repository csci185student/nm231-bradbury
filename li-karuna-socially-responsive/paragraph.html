I chose to do my project on screen addiction. These days, every app on the market is trying to hold our attention. I personally struggle with screen addiction, and I know many other people struggle with it too. This problem is an epidemic that we're not discussing because excessive screen use has been normallized in today's society. While laptops and cellphones are useful for accessing accurate information, some of the apps (such as instagram) have been known to cause body insecurities and eating disorders. Additionally, many people, myself included, suffer loss of sleep due to excessive screen usage. Losing sleep can cause a person to suffer from consequential symptoms such as fatigue, depression, anxiety, and guilt. While I don't know how exactly the issue should be solved, given our level of dependence on screens in our everday life, I believe that screen addiction is an illness that should be taken more seriously by health professionals.
